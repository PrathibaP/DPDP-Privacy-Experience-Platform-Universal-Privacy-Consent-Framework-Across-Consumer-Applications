Here's what changes

Delete listening history?

This clears the data used to personalise Discover Weekly and Home. Your playlists and saved songs are not affected. This action can't be undone.

Delete your account?

This permanently erases your profile, playlists and personal data within 30 days, as required under the DPDP Act's right to erasure. You'll lose Premium benefits immediately.

Preparing your data export

We're packaging your listening history, playlists, account details and consent log into a single file. You'll get a notification and email when it's ready — usually within 48 hours.

How long we keep your data

We keep data only as long as it's useful to you or required by law, then it's deleted automatically.

Attendance
5 years
Why: schools require attendance records for the full academic cycle. Deletion trigger: auto-purged 5 years after the record date.
Assignments
Until course ends
Why: needed for grading and progress tracking through the course. Deletion trigger: removed 90 days after course completion.
Live class recordings
Until course ends
Why: lets you revise past lessons. Deletion trigger: course completion, or earlier if a parent deletes it manually.
Quiz results
Ongoing
Why: powers weak-topic detection and personalised recommendations. Deletion trigger: removed if you turn off Learning Analytics, or on account deletion.
Payments
As required by law
Why: Indian tax law requires invoices to be retained for a fixed statutory period. Deletion trigger: cannot be deleted early, even on request.
Chat messages
12 months
Why: supports moderation and dispute resolution between students, parents, and teachers. Deletion trigger: auto-purged on a rolling 12-month window.

How Vedantu uses your learning data

We analyse quiz scores, learning progress, and class activity to personalise your study plan and recommend the next best lesson.
✓ What data is used
Quiz & test scores · Time spent per topic · Homework completion · Live class attendance
✕ What is NOT used
Camera or microphone recordings · Chat message content · Data from outside Vedantu
Why it benefits you
Surfaces the exact topics you're weak in, so revision time goes where it actually helps your score.
We never use this data for advertising, and we never sell your personal learning data.

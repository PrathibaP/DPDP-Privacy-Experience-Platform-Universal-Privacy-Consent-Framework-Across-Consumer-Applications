Before you sign up

We only ask for what we'll actually use

No single "I accept" covering everything. Each toggle you'll see next maps to one specific purpose — order fulfilment, personalization, marketing, or partner sharing.

Location is asked when it's needed

We won't ask for "Always allow" today. You'll only see a precise-location prompt the moment an order is actually being tracked.

Change your mind anytime, instantly

Every choice here lives in Privacy Hub. Download or delete your data yourself — no support ticket, no multi-day wait.
